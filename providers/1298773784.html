Hotel Contessa

TCLadybug   1 contribution
Brownwood, Texas
Feb 17, 2011 | Trip type: Business, Solo travel

We loved our stay at the Hotel Contessa! Such friendly staff all around, and the most comfortable and clean rooms we have ever stayed in. If I had to give a down side, it would be that the wireless internet was very slow in the bedroom, but in the living area it was wonderful, just like everything else! We will definitely be back!
