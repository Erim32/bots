“Indifferent to poor customer service”

Hotel Contessa

Vtravel15   1 contribution
Atlanta, Georgia
Feb 18, 2011 | Trip type: Couples

Hotel front desk was not helpful and did not seem to care. Requested early check-in, and was confirmed we could. We had the most expensive room at the hotel and all they could say was the room was large and it took a while to clean. Three hours, I don't think so. We did not get what they promised and they offered nothing as an apology. Finally we got to the room and was assured the luggage would be 'right up'. Thirty minutes later I returned to the front desk looking for our luggage. No one had a clue about the status of the luggage. I found it in a side room--with no sign of delivery anytime soon. I had to drag it upstairs myself. The only save was the conceirge who assisted.
The hotel was too far from the Riverwalk excitment, I would recommend staying somewhere else, NOT as the Hotel Contessa.
